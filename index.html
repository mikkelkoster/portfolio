About me

I care deeply about the quality of the work – and the people behind it.

The best design leaders I've encountered never fully let go of the craft — and that's always been my model. Fifteen years across consultancies and in-house roles taught me that the problems change, but the fundamentals don't: set a high bar, build the right conditions, stay close enough to the work to know when something's off. I don't lead from a distance. I'm in the details alongside my team — then stepping back to make sure the direction is still right. I've built products and led teams across Copenhagen, New York, London, and Shanghai.

What I do

01

Design at scale

Design systems built for real adoption — not just documented in Figma. Processes that hold up as teams grow. A quality bar that doesn't slip under pressure. I've scaled this at Maersk across 20+ product teams and rebuilt it from the ground up at Formalize — always starting from what the team actually needs to work well together.

02

People who thrive

I hire deliberately, mentor closely, and stay in the work alongside my team — not managing from a distance. I've led 25+ designers across agencies and in-house teams, and I still believe the most powerful thing a design leader can do is help someone grow into a version of themselves they didn't know was possible.

03

Solving hard problems

Multi-persona platforms, high-density data, complex enterprise workflows — the kind of problems where the brief itself needs redesigning before anything else. I know how to break ambiguity into clear priorities, make the case for the right approach, and keep teams moving with confidence even when the path forward isn't obvious yet.

Beyond the work

No assholes

Work is a big part of life. I refuse to spend it in toxic environments — or creating them. The culture I build is one where people feel safe, challenged, and actually want to show up.

No shortcuts on quality

There's no point putting time and effort into something if the outcome isn't exceptional. In a world where AI can generate anything, taste is the new currency. Push for quality. Be proud of the work.

Getting things done

I favour asking forgiveness over permission. I move fast and I don't wait for perfect conditions. Thoroughness and momentum aren't opposites — you just have to care enough to do both.

Tenacious, not stubborn

I challenge the status quo and say what I think — without arrogance. I'll push hard for the right solution, then listen just as hard if someone has a better one.

Positive by default

I'm a social extrovert who genuinely loves the people side of this work. I bring energy, look for the good, and believe a team that enjoys working together consistently outperforms one that doesn't.

I finish what I start

Three Ironman finishes. Competitive road cyclist in the top amateur category. I never give up — and that same relentlessness shows up in everything I build, every team I lead, every problem I refuse to leave unsolved.
